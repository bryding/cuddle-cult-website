Meet Our Camp Leaders

The passionate individuals who bring Camp Cuddle Cult to life

Ben Ryding

Camp Founder & Lead Coordinator

Ben discovered Burning Man in 2012 and was immediately drawn to its principles of radical self-expression and communal effort. After several years of participating with different camps, he founded Camp Cuddle Cult in 2018 to create a space dedicated to connection through touch and comfort.

With a background in event planning and community building, Ben oversees the overall camp logistics, infrastructure, and ensures that the camp's vision is realized each year. His passion for creating meaningful human connections drives the camp's mission and activities.

When not planning for the burn, Ben works in technology and pursues interests in meditation, contact improvisation dance, and sustainable living practices.

Playa Name: Cozy Bear

Years at Burning Man: 11

Favorite Burning Man Memory: "Facilitating our first group cuddle session at sunrise with over 50 participants, creating a beautiful web of human connection as the desert came to life around us."

Serena Lipari

Workshops & Consent Director

Serena joined Camp Cuddle Cult in its second year and quickly became an essential part of the leadership team. With her background as a somatic therapist and educator, she developed and oversees the camp's workshop program focused on consent, boundaries, and mindful touch.

Her expertise in creating safe spaces for vulnerable interactions has been instrumental in establishing the camp's reputation for respectful, consent-based activities. Serena trains all camp members in communication practices and ensures that all participants understand and honor the camp's principles.

Outside of Burning Man, Serena teaches workshops on embodiment practices and works with organizations to develop consent-based policies and cultures.

Playa Name: Gentle Touch

Years at Burning Man: 8

Favorite Burning Man Memory: "Witnessing a participant overcome their fear of physical touch through our graduated boundary exercises, and later seeing them confidently participate in group activities."

James Jenkins

Infrastructure & Technical Director

James brings his engineering expertise and creative vision to Camp Cuddle Cult, designing and building the physical structures that make our camp a comfortable haven in the harsh desert environment. He's the mastermind behind our iconic Cuddle Dome and the camp's innovative cooling systems.

With a background in sustainable design and fabrication, James ensures that our camp is not only functional but also beautiful and aligned with our values of sustainability. He leads the build team during setup and breakdown, managing the complex logistics of transporting and assembling our camp infrastructure.

In the default world, James works as an architectural designer focused on eco-friendly buildings and teaches workshops on geodesic dome construction.

Playa Name: Blueprint

Years at Burning Man: 9

Favorite Burning Man Memory: "The moment we completed the first Cuddle Dome after 24 hours of non-stop work, and seeing people's faces light up as they entered the space for the first time."

Brooke Downer

Community & Events Coordinator

Brooke is the heart and soul of Camp Cuddle Cult, fostering the sense of community that makes our camp special. She coordinates the camp's daily events schedule, evening gatherings, and special ceremonies, ensuring that there are always opportunities for meaningful connection.

With her background in performance art and community organizing, Brooke brings creativity and warmth to everything she touches. She's particularly passionate about creating rituals that help camp members process emotions and form deeper connections with each other.

When not on the playa, Brooke works as a yoga instructor and facilitates community art projects that bring people together through creative expression.

Playa Name: Ember

Years at Burning Man: 7

Favorite Burning Man Memory: "Our camp's fire circle on the night of the Temple burn, where we shared vulnerabilities and supported each other through an evening of profound emotional release and connection."
