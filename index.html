Our Story

Camp Cuddle Cult was founded in 2025 by a group of friends who shared a vision of creating a space at Burning Man dedicated to connection, openness, and dance!

We're a small group of like-minded individuals developing a vibrant community that welcomes people of all backgrounds who share our values of consent, intimacy, inclusion, and radical self-expression.

Our camp is designed as a sanctuary where participants can escape the harsh desert environment and find comfort in human connection or simple relaxation in our communal lounge spaces.

Our Principles

Consent & Boundaries

We prioritize clear communication and respect for personal boundaries in all interactions.

Radical Inclusion

Everyone is welcome regardless of background, identity, or experience.

Communal Connection

We foster meaningful relationships through shared experiences and authentic interaction.

Creative Expression

We encourage personal and collective expression through various art forms.

Physical & Emotional Safety

Creating a secure environment where everyone feels protected and valued.

Playful Participation

Active engagement and contribution to our community activities and growth.

Our Camp Features

The Cuddle Dome

Our signature geodesic dome filled with cushions, pillows, and psychedelic light show for group cuddle sessions and relaxation.

Color Dance

A dance floor lit up by giant flowers with LED's embedded in them, creating an amazing colorful dance floor.

How to Participate

Camp Cuddle Cult welcomes participants who resonate with our values and want to contribute to our community. We look for individuals who:

  • Respect the principles of consent and clear communication
  • Are willing to contribute to camp setup, maintenance, and breakdown
  • Bring a positive, inclusive attitude to all interactions
  • Have skills or offerings to share with the community
  • Are ready to both give and receive in equal measure
