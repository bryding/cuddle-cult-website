Our Story

Camp Cuddle Cult was founded in 2025 by a group of friends in the Boulder/Denver area who shared a vision of creating a space at Burning Man dedicated to connection, openness, and dance!

We welcome everyone who values consent, inclusion, and self-expression in a supportive community environment.

During the day, our camp offers a place to escape the desert heat and connect with others in our lounge spaces. At night, our dance floor lights up with giant LED flowers, creating a colorful space for dancing and celebration.

Our Camp Features

The Cuddle Dome

A cozy geodesic dome with cushions, pillows, and ambient lighting for hanging out and relaxation.

Color Dance

A dance floor illuminated by giant LED flowers, creating a vibrant and colorful nighttime experience.

Our Principles

Consent & Boundaries

We prioritize clear communication and respect for personal boundaries in all interactions.

Radical Inclusion

Everyone is welcome regardless of background, identity, or experience.

Community

We build connections through shared experiences and real conversations.

Creative Expression

We encourage personal and collective expression through various art forms.

Safety

Creating a space where everyone can feel comfortable and respected.

Playful Participation

Active engagement and contribution to our community activities and growth.

How to Participate

Camp Cuddle Cult welcomes people who share our values and want to be part of our community. We're looking for folks who:

  • Value consent and communication
  • Help with camp setup and breakdown
  • Bring a positive attitude
  • Share their skills and talents
  • Participate actively in camp life
